About Us

A top value university with more than 15,000 students , we've graduated more than 100,000 students who are leading Texas, the nation and the world.

We are a community of dreamers and doers. Talented students define their path to greatness. Alumni lead the way as they shape the world. Dedicated faculty create and share knowledge from the frontiers of discovery. Passionate staff help the students succeed.
