Academics

Get Started

The Sunray University has a tradition of giving students a sound foundation in the fundamentals of engineering but today, those fundamentals must be augmented by experiential learning and soft skills. Developing leadership and entrepreneurship skills, we ensure our students have internships to improve teamwork and communication skills, giving them real engineering experience. For those who are entrepreneurially inclined, it provides them the knowledge and resources to turn ideas into startups.

Undergraduate Programs

Information Technology

In this major, you’ll explore and develop practical skills in systems analysis and design, networking, web design, and programming. Whether you currently work in the information technology field or aspire to, the hands-on instruction and interdisciplinary approach of this program provides you with the skills needed to thrive in IT. As you progress toward your degree, you'll have the opportunity to:

  • Concentrate your coursework on a focus area such as game development, business, information security, pre-law or secondary education.
  • Complete a certification from the Committee on National Security Systems.
  • Earn a certificate in game programming.

Mechanical Engineering

A mechanical engineering education empowers students with creative thinking skills to design an exciting product or system; analytical tools to achieve their design goals; the ability to overcome all constraints; and the teamwork needed to design, market, and produce a system. As you progress toward your degree, you'll have the opportunity to:

  • Advanced manufacturing systems.
  • Advanced structural materials.
  • Conventional and alternative energy sources.

Biomedical Engineering

Biomedical engineering is the application of the principles and problem-solving techniques of engineering to biology and medicine. This is evident throughout healthcare, from diagnosis and analysis to treatment and recovery, and has entered the public conscience though the proliferation of implantable medical devices, such as pacemakers and artificial hips, to more futuristic technologies such as stem cell engineering and the 3-D printing of biological organs. As you progress toward your degree, you'll have the opportunity to:

  • Advanced manufacturing systems.
  • Advanced structural materials.
  • Conventional and alternative energy sources.

Graduate Programs

Masters in Data Science

The Master of Science in Data Science (MS-DS) program at Sunray is designed to address the current market needs for highly skilled data science and data analytics professionals. The program is designed to help graduates gain skills and experience in designing, implementing and transforming data sets into actionable knowledge. As you progress toward your degree, you'll have the opportunity to:

  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Probability and Simulation Based Inference for Data Science
  • Foundations of Regression and Predictive Modeling

Masters in Artificial Intelligence

Master in Artificial Intelligence program at Sunray University produces graduates who have exceptional technical skills in the emerging technologies of machine intelligence, and the understanding of the nature of the human environments in which the systems they build will be deployed.

  • Artificial Intelligence and Future Markets
  • AI Engineering
  • AI Innovation

Online Programs

Masters in Business Administration

The online MBA offers a comprehensive curriculum that brings the on-campus classroom environment to working professionals via online learning and streaming video. It is designed for maximum flexibility allowing you to maintain your career while obtaining a graduate degree.The online Master of Business Administration plan of study ensures that you have an understanding of specific concepts before progressing in the program.

  • Learn in a challenging academic program guided by distinguished faculty and leading practitioners.
  • Immediately apply relevant knowledge, concepts and analytical tools to on-the-job decisions.
  • Work on projects and assignments with fellow students who are experienced professionals from diverse backgrounds.

Masters in Computer Engineering

A degree in computer engineering from Sunray University will allow you to have an impact well beyond your computer screen. As devices and systems become “smarter” and more connected through the Internet of Things (IoT), computer engineers hold the key to understanding, advancing, and protecting the security of next-generation technologies. As you progress toward your degree, you'll have the opportunity to:

  • Up-to-date industry trends and technology capabilities.
  • Information that will provide specific depth in their area of focus such as high-performance computing, embedded systems, networking, etc.
  • To identify, formulate, and solve advanced engineering problems using fundamental computer engineering principles, methodologies, and tools.
