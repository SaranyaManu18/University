Master of Business Administration (MBA)

If you want to earn your Master of Business Administration from a top-ranked university on your schedule, Sunray University’s online MBA may be the perfect fit for you. In this degree, you’ll develop leadership skills and expand your knowledge of advanced business strategies.

Masters of Computer Engineering

Computer enginnering professionals are in high demand. Earning this online program will grant you the prestige and expertise to pursue the engineering field of your choice, as well as the affordability and flexibility you need in a Master's degree program.

ADMISSION CRITERIA

Learn more about the necessary prerequisites for the online Master of Engineering Management and Leadership program.

  • Bachelor’s Degree
  • GPA of 3.25 or higher
  • Work Experience(Not mandatory)
  • The strength and quality of your curriculum
  • GRE or GMAT results
  • Your talents, leadership, conduct and diversity of experience

APPLICATION CHECKLIST

  • Application fee of $85
    • The $85 application fee (or application fee waiver, if you are eligible) is required to officially submit your application for admission.
  • Graduate Admission Tests (GRE, GMAT)
    • Scores can be sent directly from the testing agency, included on official transcripts or downloaded and sent to admissions@sunray.edu
  • 2 Recommendation Letter
    • Three Letters of recommendation (should be requested within online application).
  • Official final college transcripts
    • Transcripts and degree certificates (if applicable) from all colleges/universities attended.

  • Resume
